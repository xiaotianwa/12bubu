# 单张桌宠 PNG 生成提示词

这些提示词适合逐张生成，后续再抠透明和命名入库。每张都建议用纯 `#00ff00` 背景，生成后转透明 PNG。

## idle_01

Create one full-body 2D kawaii desktop pet sprite on a perfectly flat solid #00ff00 chroma-key background. Subject: a round white panda-like baby character inspired by the references, dark chocolate ears and tiny feet, pink cheeks, dot eyes, tiny mouth, small dark bow/collar. Pose: standing front-facing, relaxed, tiny arms slightly open, soft idle expression. Style: thick rounded dark-brown outlines, flat pastel fills, sticker-like clean edges. No shadow, no text, no watermark, no props.
